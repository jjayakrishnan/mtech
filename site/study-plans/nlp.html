Key Formulas (closed book — memorize)

Chain Rule: \[ P(w_1, \ldots, w_n) = \prod_{i=1}^{n} P(w_i \mid w_1, \ldots, w_{i-1}) \] Bigram MLE: \[ P(w_i \mid w_{i-1}) = \frac{C(w_{i-1}, w_i)}{C(w_{i-1})} \] Add-1 Smoothing: \[ P^*(w_i \mid w_{i-1}) = \frac{C(w_{i-1}, w_i) + 1}{C(w_{i-1}) + V} \] Interpolation: \[ \hat{P} = \lambda_1 P_{\text{tri}} + \lambda_2 P_{\text{bi}} + \lambda_3 P_{\text{uni}} \] Perplexity: \[ PP(S) = P(S)^{-1/N} \] TF-IDF: \[ \text{tfidf}(t,d) = \text{tf}(t,d) \times \log\frac{N}{\text{df}(t)} \] Cosine Similarity: \[ \cos(\vec{A}, \vec{B}) = \frac{\vec{A} \cdot \vec{B}}{|\vec{A}|\;|\vec{B}|} \] Softmax: \[ P(w_i) = \frac{e^{z_i}}{\sum_j e^{z_j}} \] Cross-Entropy Loss: \[ L = -\sum_i y_i \log \hat{y}_i \] Viterbi Recursion: \[ v_t(j) = \max_i \bigl[v_{t-1}(i) \cdot a_{ij}\bigr] \cdot b_j(o_t) \] Precision / Recall: \[ P = \frac{TP}{TP+FP}, \quad R = \frac{TP}{TP+FN}, \quad F_1 = \frac{2PR}{P+R} \]
