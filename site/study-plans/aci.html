Key Formulas (closed book — memorize)

A* evaluation: \[ f(n) = g(n) + h(n) \] Admissibility: \[ h(n) \leq h^*(n) \] Consistency: \[ h(n) \leq c(n, n') + h(n') \] Alpha-Beta pruning: \[ \text{Prune when } \alpha \geq \beta \] ACO pheromone update: \[ \tau_{ij} \leftarrow (1 - \rho) \cdot \tau_{ij} + \Delta\tau_{ij} \] ACO edge probability: \[ p_{ij} = \frac{\tau_{ij}^\alpha \cdot \eta_{ij}^\beta}{\sum_k \tau_{ik}^\alpha \cdot \eta_{ik}^\beta} \] NEAT fitness sharing: \[ f'_i = \frac{f_i}{\sum_j \mathrm{sh}(d_{ij})} \]
